The Eminence In Shadow

Kage no Jitsuryokusha ni Naritakute!;陰の実力者になりたくて!

Type: TV

Premiered: Fall 2022

Date Aired: Oct 05, 2022 - Feb 15, 2023

Genres: Action, Comedy, Fantasy, Parody, School, Isekai, Magic

Status: Completed

Episodes: 20

Duration: 23 min

In modern-day Japan, Minoru Kageno dreams of becoming a mastermind but dies in an accident, only to be reborn as Cid Kagenou in a fantastical realm. Cid hides his ambitions and pretends to be ordinary but secretly plans to manipulate power from the shadows. When he encounters a sick elven girl named Alpha, he concocts a story about a villainous cult controlling the world. Taking on the persona of Shadow, leader of a secretive group called Shadow Garden, Cid recruits Alpha and begins a real struggle against the cult. As Shadow, Cid finds himself deeply involved in the conflicts of this world, blurring the lines between his fabricated tale and genuine threats, challenging him to live up to the role he created.
