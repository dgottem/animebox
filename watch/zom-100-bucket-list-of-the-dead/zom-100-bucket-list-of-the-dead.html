Zom 100: Bucket List of the Dead

Type: TV

Premiered: Summer 2023

Date Aired: Jul 09, 2023 - Dec 26, 2023

Genres: Action, Comedy, Horror, Seinen, Supernatural, Suspense

Status: Completed

Episodes: 12

Duration: 24 min

Akira Tendo, a disillusioned office worker at an exploitative marketing firm, finds himself trapped in a mundane existence until a zombie apocalypse strikes Tokyo. Seeing this as an opportunity to break free from his routine, he creates a bucket list of things to do before turning into a zombie, giving him a newfound sense of purpose. Joined by friends Kencho, Shizuka, and Beatrix, each seeking their own escape from their pasts, they embark on a series of adventures across zombie-infested Japan. Along the way, they encounter other survivors, face personal challenges, and learn the value of friendship and resilience in the face of adversity.
